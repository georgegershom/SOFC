================================================================================
                        📐 MATHEMATICAL MODELS INCLUDED
================================================================================

1. ARRHENIUS CONDUCTIVITY
   σ(T) = σ₀ × exp(-Eₐ / (R × T))
   → Temperature-dependent ionic conductivity

2. NORTON-BAILEY CREEP LAW
   ε̇ = B × σⁿ × exp(-Q / (R × T))
   → Time-dependent plastic deformation
   → Parameters for Ni-YSZ, 8YSZ, Crofer 22 APU

3. JOHNSON-COOK PLASTICITY
   σ_y = [A + B×εⁿ] × [1 + C×ln(ε̇*)] × [1 - T*ᵐ]
   → Strain hardening, rate sensitivity, thermal softening
   → Complete parameters for Ni-YSZ

4. BUTLER-VOLMER KINETICS
   i = 2 × i₀ × sinh(F × η / (2 × R × T))
   → Electrode reaction kinetics
   → Exchange currents for all electrodes
